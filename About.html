Our Story

APAC was founded in 2019 by a group of students who were passionate about data analytics. They wanted to create a platform for students to learn and grow together in the field of data analytics.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a platform for students to learn and grow together in the field of data analytics. We aim to provide students with the necessary skills and knowledge to prepare them for the industry.
